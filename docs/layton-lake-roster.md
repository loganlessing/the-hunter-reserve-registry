## Great Ones

**Whitetail Deer** — Class 4 · Level 10 Fabled
- Max-level-3 species: thin the low levels, stack the level 3s; rest to cycle need zones. Was the *first* species ever to get a Great One.
- Fabled coats: Brown, Piebald, Tan

**Black Bear** — Class 7 · Level 10 Fabled
- Max-level-9 species: never shoot levels 1–4, stack the 9s. Predator caller pulls them in; they feed early morning and late night. G1 reaches 316 kg / 697 lbs.
- Fabled coats: Chestnut, Cream, Glacier (×2), Spirit, Spotted

**Moose** — Class 8 · Level 10 Fabled
- Max-level-5 species: shoot level 3–4 bulls, leave 1–2, stack the 5s. Work the lakeshore drink-to-bedding paths. G1 reaches 700 kg / 1,543 lbs.
- Fabled coats: Ashen, Birch, Oak, Speckled, Spruce, Two Tone

*Great One spawn odds are unofficial community estimates — treat it as a numbers game.*

## Accuracy notes
- Classes, weights, medal scores, zone times, callers and coats are from the thehuntercotw structured sheet, cross-checked against the COTW Wiki.
- **Weights are max only** (the value shown on spotting) — written as "up to X," no invented minimums.
- **Flag — Whitetail Deer max level:** the source sheet lists Whitetail's max level as **3** (lower than the other deer, which are 5). Diamonds therefore come off level 3. This is the one value worth a quick in-game confirm, since it's unusually low; everything else cross-checked cleanly.
- Great One spawn odds are unofficial community estimates.
