## Great Ones (six — the most of any base reserve)

**Ring-Necked Pheasant** — Class 1 · Level 10 Fabled (added Dec 2024)
- Max-level-3, and it must be shot in flight — the trickiest of the six. Thin low levels, stack the 3s.
- Fabled coats (gemstone set): Citrine, Emerald, Garnet, Morganite, Obsidian, Pearl, Ruby, Sapphire

**Red Fox** — Class 2 · Level 10 Fabled (added Dec 2024)
- Max-level-9: never shoot levels 1–4, stack the 9s. Jackrabbit predator caller keeps them coming.
- Fabled coats: Blood Moon, Candycane, Cherry Blossom, Licorice, Midnight Poppy, Mystic Snowdrop, Peppermint, Rosebud Frost, Scarlet Nightshade

**Roe Deer** — Class 3 · Level 10 Fabled (added 14 Apr 2026, Patch 9.1)
- Max-level-3: thin low levels, stack the 3s. Newest Great One in the game.
- Fabled coats: Kindledawn, Wildfire, Ghostveil, Moonmarked, Mistbound, Rustveil

**Fallow Deer** — Class 4 · Level 10 Fabled (added Jun 2023)
- Max-level-5: shoot level 3–4, stack the 5s. G1 reaches 140 kg / 308 lbs.
- Fabled coats: Golden, Hooded, Mocha, Painted, Silver

**Wild Boar** — Class 5 · Level 10 Fabled (added 9 Dec 2025)
- Max-level-5: shoot level 3–4, stack the 5s. Watch for charges during the grind.
- Fabled coats: Ash, Brindle, Butterscotch, Chalk, Cinder, Scorch, Smolder, Stitch, Stripple

**Red Deer** — Class 6 · Level 10 Fabled
- Max-level-9: never shoot levels 1–4, stack the 9s. G1 reaches 260 kg / 573 lbs.
- Fabled coats: Spotted *(Red Deer Great Ones classically appear only in Fabled Spotted; the Dec 2025 remodel may have expanded this — verify in-game if it matters to you).*

*Great One spawn odds are unofficial community estimates — treat it as a numbers game.*

## Accuracy notes
- Classes, weights, medal scores, zone times, callers and coats are from the thehuntercotw structured sheet, cross-checked against the COTW Wiki.
- **Weights are max only** (spotting value) — written as "up to X," no invented minimums.
- **Great Ones:** the source stat sheet predates several additions, so I confirmed the current six against the wiki and patch notes — Red Fox & Pheasant (Dec 2024, Salzwiesen), Wild Boar (Dec 2025, Tòrr nan Sithean), Roe Deer (Apr 2026, Patch 9.1), plus the long-standing Fallow Deer and Red Deer.
- **Red Deer Fabled furs:** historically only Fabled Spotted; a Dec 2025 remodel *may* have added more — flagged rather than guessed.
- Great One spawn odds are unofficial community estimates.
