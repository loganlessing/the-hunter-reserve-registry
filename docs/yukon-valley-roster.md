## Great Ones

Yukon Valley is a three–Great One reserve. Each spawns a Level 10 **Fabled** tier above its normal ladder; the level ladder on each card renders the extra orange Fabled cell.

**Red Fox** — Class 2 · Level 10 Fabled
**Gray Wolf** — Class 6 · Level 10 Fabled
**Moose** — Class 8 · Level 10 Fabled

*Per–Great One Fabled coat lists and herd-management grind notes aren't in the current Yukon dataset — unlike the Silver Ridge roster, this data was never captured in `yukon-valley.ts`. Flagged here as **to be added from verified sources** rather than filled in from memory, per the reserve's data policy.*

*Great One spawn odds are unofficial — community estimates, not a guaranteed rate. Treat it as a numbers game.*

## Accuracy notes
- **Classes, max/Diamond levels, weights, medal scores, zone times, callers, coats, and hot spots** are transcribed verbatim from `src/data/reserves/yukon-valley.ts`, which was verified against the COTW Wiki, the thehuntercotw zone-time sheet, and community location/fur/weight data (Dec 2025 patch).
- **Weight ranges** reflect the upper (trophy-relevant) portion of each species' distribution, not newborn/runt minimums.
- **Medal tiers** by % of max trophy rating: Bronze <20% · Silver 20–60% · Gold 60–90% · Diamond ≥90%. Diamond spawns at max level or one below. The Bronze `<` prefix marks it as an open lower bound, matching the card's score row.
- **Grizzly Bear** reaches Level 9 but has **no** Great One — Yukon's Great Ones are Moose, Gray Wolf, and Red Fox only.
- **Great One Fabled coats and grind/herd-management notes** were never captured in the Yukon dataset; they're flagged as to-be-added rather than invented, per the reserve's verified-data-only policy.
- **Great One spawn odds** are unofficial community estimates.
